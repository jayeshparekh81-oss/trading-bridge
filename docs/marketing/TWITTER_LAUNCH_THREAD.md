   - "Bhai, India ka algo-trading scene ka biggest problem..." (used — direct, bhai-tone)
   - "Maine 6 algo subscriptions try kiye 2024 mein. Sab loss." (founder-story-first; pivots to product in tweet 2)

   Two of the 6 subscriptions were industry-famous bait products. Naming them
   adds credibility but invites a legal threat. Recommendation: keep
   anonymous in the launch thread; expand in a follow-up blog post.

   (cleanest), 🛠️ (engineer-built), 📊 (data/ledger). Recommendation: 🇮🇳
   for the launch (Indian retail audience signal).

   ("ye sab toh Zerodha ne pehle se diya hai", "kitna paisa raise kiya
   tha?", "show audit reports"). Founder needs a 5-bullet reply playbook
